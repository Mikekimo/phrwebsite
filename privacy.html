Privacy Policy

At Pure Heart Relief, we are committed to respecting and protecting the privacy of our donors, supporters, and website visitors. We collect essential personal information to process donations, issue tax receipts, and provide services to those in need. Rest assured, we never sell your data and only use it for legitimate charitable purposes.

Information we collect from you

We may collect personal information such as name, address, email address, and payment details when you make a donation or interact with us. This information is crucial for processing donations and providing you with the necessary documentation.

Access to your Personal Information

You have the right to access, update, and correct your personal information. If you need assistance with this, please reach out to us at [email protected].

How we use your information

We use the information you provide to process donations, issue tax receipts, and keep you informed about our charitable activities. Your data is never shared with third parties for commercial purposes.

Storage and Security of your information

We take the security of your personal information seriously and have implemented measures to protect it from unauthorized access. Your data is stored securely and only accessed by authorized personnel.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze how visitors interact with our site. These tools help us improve our services and tailor our content to better meet your needs.
