Questions

How can I contribute to Pure Heart Relief?
You can contribute by making donations, signing up as a volunteer, or staying updated on our events and news.
Are donations tax-deductible?

At this time, donations to Pure Heart Relief are not yet eligible for official tax receipts as we are in the process of developing and growing our organization. Our goal is to work toward becoming a registered charity in the future.

Can I donate items directly?
Yes, you can donate essential items directly to Pure Heart Relief to support our poverty relief efforts.
