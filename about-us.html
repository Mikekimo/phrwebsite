About Us

Pure Heart Relief is a Canadian registered charity based in Woodstock, Ontario, dedicated to reducing poverty and supporting individuals and families facing financial hardship. We provide essential items that help people meet their basic needs and live with dignity.

Our support includes food assistance, hygiene products, clean water support, clothing, seasonal essentials, and basic household goods. All our programs are designed to respond to real needs within the community.

Pure Heart Relief serves vulnerable populations affected by poverty and social exclusion, including low-income families, seniors on fixed incomes, single parents, newcomers and refugees, people with disabilities, the working poor, at-risk youth, and individuals experiencing homelessness or crisis situations. Our work is rooted in respect, inclusion, and compassion.

We collaborate closely with community partners, volunteers, and donors to deliver assistance that is accessible, practical, and culturally sensitive. Pure Heart Relief is an officially registered Canadian charity and is authorized to issue tax receipts for eligible donations. Through community-driven action, we aim to reduce the impact of poverty and strengthen well-being—one person and one family at a time.

Trusted by Many

Pure Heart Relief is proud to be supported by a growing community of donors, volunteers, and partners who share our mission.

Our Beliefs

We believe in the power of collective action and the importance of providing essential items to alleviate poverty. Every person deserves dignity, stability, and the opportunity to live with confidence and hope.
